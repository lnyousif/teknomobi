We are a .NET agency that practices agile development to build your service.

Our main goals are to engage you into a partnership, built on trust and respect, and to work together as a team.

Transparency, flexibility and product quality are the key, so we designed our collaborative processes around test-driven development, strict unit tests, rigorous peer reviews and QA testing.

Initial analysis

Problem analysis based on the interview with client. Describing needs and requirements.

Project analysis

Our developer will divide your idea into small tasks and estimate how much time it will take.

Implementation

We use Scrum, Kanban or lean methodologies. We take care about the quality of your project so that it will be easily maintainable. We use BDD (form of TDD) focused on integration testing – outside-in approach. We use GIT repository and own CI server. Every change is reviewed by second experienced developer.

Maintenance and service

We provide professional maintenance and service. We focus on bugs fixing, modifying, upgrading and refactoring.
